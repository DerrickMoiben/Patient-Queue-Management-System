Platform

Everything a modern hospital workflow needs

One system that connects every department involved in a patient's visit, from the front desk to the pharmacy counter.

Patient Registration

Capture demographics and visit details once, and reuse them across every department automatically.

Digital Queue Management

Replace paper tickets with auto-generated queue numbers that move patients through each stage in order.

Reception Dashboard

Front-desk staff see arrivals, queue length and department load at a glance, in real time.

Triage Management

Prioritize patients by urgency with vitals capture and configurable triage categories.

Doctor Consultation

Doctors call the next patient, view history and route to lab or pharmacy with one click.

Laboratory Workflow

Lab requests, sample tracking and result upload stay linked to the patient's active queue entry.

Pharmacy Workflow

Dispensing queues sync with prescriptions the moment a doctor completes a consultation.

Live Queue Display TVs

Waiting areas show now-serving numbers per department on a clean, easy-to-read public display.

Role-Based Access Control

Reception, triage, doctors, lab and pharmacy each see only the tools relevant to their role.

Real-Time Patient Tracking

Know exactly where every patient is in their visit, from check-in to discharge.

Reporting & Analytics

Track wait times, department load and daily volumes with exportable reports for management.

Secure Patient Records

Encrypted storage and audit trails keep patient data protected and compliant.
